Not Every Strategy Can Be Automated

A profitable strategy does not automatically mean it can be transformed into a reliable trading bot. Many trading systems depend on parameters that require human discretion:

  • Manual chart interpretation
  • Subjective decision making
  • Visual pattern recognition
  • Discretionary trade management

These factors can make automation difficult or impossible. Before development begins, we evaluate whether your strategy is truly suitable for automated execution.
